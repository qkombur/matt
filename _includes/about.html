About me

"I am first an engineer, but I have a degree in Communication Design and a high degree of talent in fine art and creative media in general. I didn't realize my dream goal until after I had tried my hand at many things including: two degrees, multiple employment changes, and several engineering positions. After all those things, I realized I just wanted to be the creative guy I was always meant to be. I wanted to make toys, comics, games, animation, music and more. So that's exactly what I set out to do. I know what you're thinking: there isn't much here now. But things will change quickly. So sit back and enjoy. The only limit is the imagination."
